How to Score 95+ in CBSE Accountancy

Scoring 95+ in Accountancy is not about studying more hours randomly. It is about building concepts, practising formats, reducing calculation mistakes and revising repeatedly before exams.

Master Journal Entries

Most chapters become easier when the logic of debit and credit is clear.

Write Working Notes Clearly

Working notes can save marks when final answers are complex.

Practise Timed Papers

Speed matters. Students should solve full papers before board exams.

Accountancy Study Plan

Weekly Routine

  • Revise formulas and formats
  • Solve numerical questions daily
  • Maintain a mistake notebook
  • Attempt chapter tests

Exam Routine

  • Read questions carefully
  • Show working notes
  • Use correct formats
  • Keep time for checking
